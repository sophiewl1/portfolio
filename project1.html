Triptique

In this group project, we tackled the self-chosen topic of "Mental Health," creating a series of cohesive visual designs for a theater, concert, and reading event at the fictitious venue "Triptyque." This project allowed us to explore the emotional depth and complexities of mental health through creative media, aiming to spark conversation and encourage empathy.

Our work included not only individual posters but also a large-format banner, a dynamic social media presence, and a uniquely designed flyer that doubled as a poster on the reverse side. The heart of our design approach lay in the expressive, handwritten quotes from the plays themselves that were meant to evoke curiosity. We paired these texts with a series of photographs we took ourselves, capturing both the haunting and hopeful aspects of the theme.

To add a layer of tactile authenticity, we printed these images, tore and crumpled them by hand, and then digitized them again, creating textures that visually represented the fragmentation and resilience that often accompany mental health journeys.
