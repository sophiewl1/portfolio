I grew up in a small town and discovered my passion for painting early on. I often spent hours with brushes and canvases, and I regularly attended an art school to improve my skills. After the 10th grade, I decided to switch from a traditional high school to a technical high school to focus on my specialization in Design and Media Technology. Even back then, I knew I wanted to pursue a creative career.

After graduating from high school, I completed a two-year apprenticeship as a media designer at a medium-sized advertising agency. This time was very educational, and I gained valuable practical experience. During my apprenticeship, I knew I wanted to study further, but I was still unsure about the specific field.

When I discovered the University of Media, I immediately knew it was the right choice for me. I was thrilled when I received my acceptance letter. I moved to Stuttgart for my studies and quickly felt at home there. I thoroughly enjoy my studies, as they involve many creative aspects and focus heavily on user experience.

During my practical semester, I had the opportunity to work at a renowned advertising agency in Stuttgart, which is the lead agency for Mercedes Benz Vans. This experience was incredibly enriching and further enhanced my practical skills. Now, I am in my sixth semester and look forward to expanding my knowledge and skills even further through a semester abroad.

My journey has been marked by many creative milestones, and I am excited to see what new opportunities and challenges the future holds for me.
